Dylan Quinn

Sports Media Director & Cinematographer

Dylan Quinn is a sports media director and cinematographer based in the United States.

His work focuses on the moments around the game — the quiet pre-dawn hours, the long bus rides, the post-whistle stillness — and the people who carry them.

Recent collaborators include national outlets and brands working in football, basketball, soccer, and endurance sports.
